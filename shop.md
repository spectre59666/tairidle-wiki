# Shop Locations

All purchasable items organized by category and shop location.

**Categories:**
- **Items** — general consumable and battle items
- **Balls** — Poké Balls and variants
- **TMs** — Technical Machines
- **Evolution Items** — HP and status recovery items
- **Held Items** — Items that can be held by pokemons to get a boost or evolve
